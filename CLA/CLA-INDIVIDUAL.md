## How to sign

1. Open a pull request to the Project on GitHub.
2. When the CLA Assistant bot comments on your PR, follow the link and sign with your GitHub account. Your signature is recorded against your GitHub user identity and applies to all future contributions automatically.

Until the CLA Assistant bot is configured, you may sign by adding the following line to your pull-request description:

```
I have read and agree to the SMAP Individual CLA v1.0.
Signed-off-by: <Your Full Name> <your-email@example.com>
```

For corporate contributions on behalf of an employer, please use [CLA-ENTITY.md](./CLA-ENTITY.md) instead.
